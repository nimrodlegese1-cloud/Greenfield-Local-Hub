About us
Greenfield Local Hub connects customers with local farms and provides full traceability of food products. Our system allows users to view where products are grown, supporting transparency and trust. By working with local farmers, we promote sustainable agriculture, reduce food miles, and deliver fresh, high-quality produce directly to the community.
